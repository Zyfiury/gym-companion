Privacy Policy

Gym Companion — Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

  • Account: email address, display name, authentication identifiers (Firebase Auth)
  • Health & fitness: step count (via Health Connect, with your permission), workout logs, weight history
  • Nutrition: food logs, macro targets, meal plans, photos you submit for calorie estimation
  • Location: approximate GPS when you request nearby supermarkets or delivery (with permission)
  • Usage: crash reports and analytics (Firebase Crashlytics / Analytics)

How we use data

To provide personalised workouts, meal plans, AI coaching, progress tracking, and subscription features. AI features use Groq to process your messages and food descriptions. Food photos may be sent to Google Cloud Vision for recognition.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase (auth, database, messaging, crash reporting)
  • Groq (AI coach responses)
  • Google Cloud Vision (optional food photo recognition)
  • Google Places (location-based store search)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • YouTube (recipe and exercise videos)

Data storage & security

Your profile and progress are stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We do not sell your personal data.

Your rights

You can delete your account and associated data from the Profile screen in the app. For data requests, contact support@gymcompanion.app.

Children

Gym Companion is not intended for users under 16.
