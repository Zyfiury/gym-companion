The core loop

Three things, done properly

Most apps do one of these well. Gym Companion was built so each one makes the others smarter.

Nutrition

Log food before your fork hits the plate

No more searching "chicken breast" through 400 duplicate entries. Point your camera at the meal and you're done.

  • Photo AI - calories, protein, carbs, fat and micros from a single snap
  • Barcode + verified database - accuracy badges so you know what's exact vs estimated
  • Voice logging - "two eggs and toast" and it's logged to the right meal
  • Editable everything - fix servings and meal slots after the fact

AI Coach

Mara knows what you ate, lifted and slept

Generic chatbots give generic advice. Mara reads your real logs - so "what should I eat tonight?" gets an answer that fits your remaining macros and your budget.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How accurate is the photo food logging?
Photo estimates are AI-generated and clearly badged as "Estimated" - you can adjust servings in one tap. Barcode scans and database foods are badged "Verified". You always know which is which.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit, never sold, and never used for advertising. You can delete your account and all data from inside the app at any time. Full details in our privacy policy.
What makes Mara different from ChatGPT?
Context. Mara sees your meals, workouts, weight trend, streak and goals - so her advice is about your day, not a generic answer. She can also take action: log food, swap meals and update your plan directly from chat.
Do I need to pay to use it?
No - food logging, workout plans, live sessions and the coach (10 messages/day) are free. Pro unlocks unlimited coaching and premium analytics. Waitlist members get Pro free during the beta.
When does it launch? iOS?
Android first via Google Play - waitlist members get the first invites. iOS follows after the Android launch settles.
