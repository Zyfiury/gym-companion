Privacy Policy

Gym Companion · Operated by Gym Companion · Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gym Companion ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Who we are

Gym Companion is a fitness and nutrition companion app. For privacy enquiries, contact us at support@gymcompanion.app.

2. Data we collect

3. How we use your data

  • Provide and personalise workouts, meal plans, macro targets, and progress tracking
  • Power the AI coach with context about your goals, logs, and restrictions
  • Recognise food from photos and barcodes to help you log nutrition
  • Find nearby stores and restaurants when you request location-based features
  • Manage Gym Companion Pro subscriptions and restore purchases
  • Improve reliability, fix bugs, and understand feature usage
  • Send optional push notifications (e.g. reminders), if enabled

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are in the UK or EEA, we process personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract: to provide the app and your account (profile, plans, logs)
  • Legitimate interests: analytics, fraud prevention, service improvement (balanced against your rights)
  • Consent: location access, camera/photos, Health Connect, push notifications - you may withdraw consent in device or app settings
  • Legal obligation: where required to comply with applicable law

5. Third-party services

We use trusted processors. They access data only to perform services on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase - authentication, cloud database (Firestore), analytics, crash reporting, push messaging
  • Groq - AI coach message processing
  • Google Cloud Vision - food photo recognition (when you scan meals)
  • Google Places - supermarket and restaurant search near you
  • YouTube Data API - exercise and recipe video links
  • RevenueCat - subscription and entitlement management
  • Google Play - payment processing for subscriptions

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data storage & security

  • Account and progress data are stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Profile photos may be stored locally on your device
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • We apply access controls and industry-standard safeguards; no method is 100% secure

7. Data retention

  • Account and fitness data: retained while your account is active
  • After account deletion: we delete or anonymise associated data within a reasonable period, except where law requires retention
  • Analytics and crash logs: retained per Firebase default retention settings

8. Your rights

UK / EEA (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access - request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - correct inaccurate data in the app or by contacting us
  • Erasure - delete your account in Profile → Account → Delete account
  • Restriction - ask us to limit processing in certain cases
  • Data portability - export your data as CSV/PDF from Profile (Pro feature), or email support@gymcompanion.app for a portable copy
  • Object - object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent - for location, camera, health, or notifications via device settings
  • Complain - to the ICO (UK) or your local supervisory authority

California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used (see sections above)
  • Delete personal information - use in-app account deletion or email us
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of sale/share - we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising
  • Non-discrimination - we will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights

To exercise CCPA rights, email support@gymcompanion.app with subject line "California Privacy Request". We will verify your request within the timeframes required by law.

9. International transfers

Some processors (including Firebase, Groq, and RevenueCat) are based in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms provided by our vendors.

10. Children's privacy

Gym Companion is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided data and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
